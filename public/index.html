Wildcat: AI Tools for Twitch Streamers

Knowledgeable chatbots, realistic TTS, and smart overlays – all in one platform

Launch WildcatSage Launch WildcatTTS

Wildcat is a suite of AI-driven Twitch bot tools designed to elevate your stream's interactivity. From answering chat questions intelligently to reading messages aloud with natural TTS and generating on-brand chat overlays, Wildcat brings advanced AI to your channel with minimal setup.

All tools are seamlessly integrated and maintained under the Wildcat platform, making it one cohesive ecosystem for your streaming needs.

Wildcat Sage

An AI-powered chatbot that lives in your Twitch chat. Sage can answer viewer questions, fetch information from the web, play trivia and mini-games, and even translate or role-play – all automatically in chat.

  • Contextual AI responses powered by Google Gemini
  • Real-time web search integration
  • Interactive games (trivia, geo-guessing, riddles)
  • Auto-chat and multi-language translation
  • Customizable commands and behavior

Wildcat TTS

Give your chat a voice with WildcatTTS. This text-to-speech bot reads chat messages and alerts aloud in real time using natural-sounding AI voices. Engage your audience with hundreds of voice options!

  • Natural-sounding TTS with 300+ voices
  • Channel points and Bits integration
  • AI music generation capabilities
  • Web dashboard for easy configuration
  • OBS overlay support for visual feedback

Wildcat Chat Overlay

A customizable Twitch chat overlay with AI-generated themes. Describe any aesthetic (e.g. "cyberpunk city" or "cozy forest") and let the AI design a matching background image and style for your chat. No login required – just plug into OBS!

  • AI-powered theme generation
  • Visual editor for colors, fonts, and layout
  • Multiple display modes (standard and pop-ups)
  • No authentication needed
  • Instant OBS integration via URL
