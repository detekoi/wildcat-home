Wildcat: Unique Tools for Twitch Streamers

Knowledgeable chatbots, realistic TTS, and smart overlays – all in one platform

Wildcat is a suite of Twitch & YouTube bot tools designed to elevate your stream's interactivity. From answering chat questions intelligently to reading messages aloud with natural TTS and generating on-brand chat overlays, Wildcat brings advanced AI to your channel with minimal setup.

Explore Wildcat Tools

Wildcat Sage

A Google Gemini-powered chatbot that lives in your Twitch chat. Sage can answer viewer questions, fetch information from the web, play trivia and mini-games, and even translate or role-play – all automatically in chat.

  • Contextual responses powered by Google Gemini
  • Real-time web search integration
  • Interactive games (trivia, geo-guessing, riddles)
  • Auto-chat and multi-language translation
  • Customizable commands and behavior

Wildcat TTS

Give your chat a voice with WildcatTTS. This text-to-speech bot reads chat messages and alerts aloud in real time using natural-sounding AI voices. Engage your audience with hundreds of voice options!

  • Natural-sounding TTS with 300+ voices
  • Channel points and Bits integration
  • Web dashboard for easy configuration
  • Customizable viewer voice preferences

Wildcat Chat Overlay

A customizable Twitch & YouTube chat overlay with themes generated using natural language. Describe any aesthetic (e.g. "cyberpunk city" or "cozy forest") and let the AI design a matching background image and style for your chat. No login required – just plug into OBS!

  • Automatic theme generation
  • Visual editor for colors, fonts, and layout
  • Multiple display modes (standard and pop-ups)
  • No authentication needed
  • Instant OBS integration via URL
