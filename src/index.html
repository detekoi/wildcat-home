Wildcat is a suite of Twitch & YouTube bot tools designed to elevate your stream's interactivity. From answering chat questions intelligently to reading messages aloud with natural TTS and generating on-brand chat overlays, Wildcat brings advanced interactivity and customization to your channel with minimal setup.

All tools are seamlessly integrated and maintained under the Wildcat platform, making it one cohesive ecosystem for your streaming needs. Currently in invite-only beta — request access.

A Google Gemini-powered chatbot that lives in your Twitch chat. Sage answers viewer questions, fetches information from the web, runs trivia, and translates.
